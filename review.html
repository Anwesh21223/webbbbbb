By Anwesh Shrestha

// Book
Elon Musk
Walter Isaacson
★★★★★

Walter Isaacson is one of my favourite authors. He writes biographies — and he writes them like no one else. Raw, unfiltered, and deeply researched. His biography of Elon Musk is perhaps his most ambitious yet: a meticulous account of the life of one of the most consequential figures in the advancement of human engineering and the expansion of what we believe is possible. I finished all 600+ pages in one week — not because I had to, but because I simply could not stop. What makes Isaacson's work rare in this era is something worth noting: the subjects of his biographies have no permission to read, let alone influence, the final manuscript. In a world where narratives are constantly managed and curated, that kind of editorial independence is almost frightening in the best way. This book is essential reading.
